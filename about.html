About

     I started Cifuentes Consulting, LLC with the ambition of growing my company in unison with my clients’ businesses. I attended Le Moyne College, located in Syracuse, New York, between 2010-2015 where I worked towards my Bachelors in Accountancy and MBA.
    In 2015 I started my public accountant journey working at The Bonadio Group, a top 50 accounting firm, headquartered in Rochester, NY. In 2019 I joined The Red Tie Society, an advisory board for the Ronald McDonald House Charities of Greater Rochester, as the Marketing Chair, where I assisted with fundraising and charity work for the organization.
    In 2021 I moved to Denver, Colorado where I found the love of my life I started Cifuentes Consulting, LLC. , I decided it was time to utilize my knowledge and experience in a different capacity. Today I’m here ready to help bring your business to the next level.
