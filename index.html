We work with small businesses and organization to help position themselves for growth. By focusing on your accounting and finance function, we save you time and energy to concentrate on the important day-to-day items. As a trusted advisor, you can collaborate with an experienced professional with industry specialization and develop your business. From core accounting and finance functions to strategic consulting, we can help.

Challenges You Face

Most organizations contend with limited resources and expanding demands. As your needs change over time, it can be challenging to meet those requirements with your existing resources. We can help fill your needs that might emerge as your organization grows.

In-house turnover can affect your short and long-term health. We provide stability in staffing without spending extra time and resources searching for replacements.

Your finance and accounting function should do more than just process transactions and produce financial reporting.  It should produce insight and drive the actions that will impact your organization’s operations and profitability.

Bookkeeper

  • Accounts payable
  • Accounts receivable
  • 1099 set up & management
  • Bank reconcilation
  • Basic QuickBooks manangement

Senior Accountant

Bookkeeper services including:

  • Standard financial statements
  • Fixed assets reconciliations
  • General ledger management
  • Intermediate QuickBooks management

Controller

Senior accountant services including:

  • Internal controls set up & integration
  • Cash flow management and planning
  • Custom management reports
  • Advanced QuickBooks management
  • Inventory management and implementation

CFO

CFO services including:

  • Annual, quarterly, and monthly budgeting and forecasting
  • Develop and analyze key performance indicators (KPI’s)
  • Manage and maintain banking relationships
  • Board meeting presentations
  • Strategic planning
